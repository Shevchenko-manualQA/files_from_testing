## [EXE-5] The product image in the catalog is not loaded (the list of products is represented by cells)

**Severity:** Sev-3  
**Priority:** Low  

### Environment
- Windows 10, Google Chrome 123.0.6
- Android 13

### Preconditions
- Open the website https://exe.ua/ua/ in a browser

### Steps
1.	Click on “Каталог“(Catalog).
2.	Navigate to the “Програмне забезпечення“ (Software) category.
3.	Go to subcategory “Офісні програми“(Office software).
4.	Find “Microsoft Office 2021“ in the list of products.
5.	Observe the image.

### Expected Result
The product image for “Microsoft Office 2021“ is properly displayed.

### Actual Result
The product image for “Microsoft Office 2021” is not displayed (the image does not load).
