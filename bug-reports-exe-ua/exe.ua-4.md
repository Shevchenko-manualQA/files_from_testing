## [EXE-4] Delivery, payment, and reviews block is misplaced on the product page 

**Severity:** Sev-3  
**Priority:** Low  

### Environment
- Windows 10, Google Chrome 123.0.6
- Android 13

### Preconditions
- Open the website https://exe.ua/ua/ in a browser

### Steps
1.	Click on “Каталог“(Catalog).
2.	Navigate to the “Смартфони, телефони“ (Smartphones, Phones) category.
3.	Go to subcategory “Чохли для мобільних телефонів“ (Mobile Phone Cases).
4.	Select "Чохол для смартф. SAMSUNG для A55 Smart View Wallet Case EF-ZA556CBEGWW / Black".
5.	Observe the block with information about delivery, payment, and reviews.

### Expected Result
The block with information about delivery, payment, and reviews is located on the right side of the screen.

### Actual Result
The block with information about delivery, payment, and reviews is located on the left side, below the image.
